About us

Making Education Accessible for All

Empowering Lives Through Compassion.

Aliff Foundation is the social initiative of Aliff Overseas Pvt Ltd, committed to championing girl child education as a top priority. We believe that when you educate a girl, you uplift an entire community. Our mission is to break barriers that prevent young girls from accessing quality education by providing scholarships, school supplies, mentorship, and career guidance. Alongside this, we work to empower underprivileged communities through educational aid and skill development programs. By ensuring equal learning opportunities, we help young minds—especially girls—build a brighter and more independent future. With compassion and dedication, Aliff Foundation is on a mission to make education a reality for every child, one step at a time.
